About Us

“Craftsmanship names an enduring, basic human impulse: the desire to do a job well for its own sake.” – Richard Sennett

Who we are

New Mexico Forge & Wood was born out of a passion for craftsmanship and a deep respect for honest, hands-on work. Founded in 2024, we bring together over a decade of combined experience in both woodworking and metalworking.

After four years working professionally as a welder, I set out to create custom furniture that merges the raw strength of forged metal with the natural warmth of hand-worked wood. Along the way, I teamed up with a skilled partner I met through work — someone who, like me, shares a strong background in welding and years of experience in fine woodworking.

Together, we craft pieces that are durable, functional, and beautiful — built to last and made with care right here in New Mexico. Whether it's a custom table, a welded frame, or a handmade home accent, every item is a reflection of the pride we take in our work.

At New Mexico Forge & Wood, we don’t just build furniture — we build pieces with purpose, character, and craftsmanship.
