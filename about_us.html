About Us

“Craftsmanship names an enduring, basic human impulse: the desire to do a

job well for its own sake.” – Richard Sennett

Who we are

New Mexico Forge & Wood was born out of a passion for craftsmanship and a deep respect for honest, hands-on work. Founded in 2024, we bring together over a decade of combined experience in both woodworking and metalworking.

After four years working professionally as a welder, I set out to create custom furniture that merges the raw strength of forged metal with the natural warmth of hand-worked wood. Along the way, I teamed up with a skilled partner I met through work — someone who, like me, shares a strong background in welding and years of experience in fine woodworking.

Together, we craft pieces that are durable, functional, and beautiful — built to last and made with care right here in New Mexico. Whether it's a custom table, a welded frame, or a handmade home accent, every item is a reflection of the pride we take in our work.

At New Mexico Forge & Wood, we don’t just build furniture — we build pieces with purpose, character, and craftsmanship.

What we offer

We specialize in custom-built furniture, handcrafted with a blend of wood and metal to suit any space — modern, rustic, industrial, or something entirely unique. Our work ranges from functional statement pieces to one-of-a-kind home accents, all made with precision and care.

Here's what we currently offer:

  • Custom Wood Furniture — Dining tables, benches, shelves, desks, and more. All built to order, using high-quality hardwoods and detailed finishes.
  • Metal Fabrication & Design — Clean welds, strong structures, and stylish frames. We bring a welder’s eye for durability into every piece.
  • Mixed-Media Builds — Seamless combinations of steel, iron, and natural wood that bring a unique edge to any environment.
  • Commission Work — Got an idea in mind? We love working with clients to turn sketches and vision boards into something real.
  • Small-Batch Pieces — From time to time, we offer ready-made items for sale — all handcrafted, never mass-produced.
